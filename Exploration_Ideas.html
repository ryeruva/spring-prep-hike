Proposed Destinations

🏝️
Multi-Day Islands

Channel Islands

Backpacking to Santa Rosa or Santa Cruz Island with pristine beaches, coastal trails, and unique marine wildlife viewing opportunities.

Difficulty: Advanced
Distance: Variable
🏜️
Desert Spring/Fall

Death Valley Low Desert

Spring or fall desert camping with badlands hiking, slot canyons, and ranger-led education programs exploring desert ecology.

Difficulty: Moderate
Distance: 15-20mi
🏔️
Alpine Lakes

Mokelumne Wilderness

High alpine lakes backpacking in the central Sierra, featuring pristine water systems, technical scrambles, and remote camping.

Difficulty: Advanced
Distance: 25-30mi
🌵
Rock Climbing Desert

Joshua Tree Rock Climbing

Technical rock climbing camp with bouldering and sport climbing instruction, desert survival skills, and outdoor education.

Difficulty: Intermediate
Activity: Climbing
🚣
Kayaking Redwoods

Humboldt Lagoons Kayaking

Paddling adventure through pristine lagoons surrounded by ancient redwood groves with wildlife observation and coastal exploration.

Difficulty: Moderate
Activity: Kayaking
🏜️
Multi-Day Canyons

Canyonlands Technical Backpack

Multi-day backpacking expedition through Utah's dramatic canyon country with slot canyons, arches, and advanced navigation skills.

Difficulty: Expert
Distance: 30+ mi
