Bear Can Packing Guide

How to Pack Your Bear Can

Use three sizes of zip-lock bags. Pack Monday first so Saturday sits on top — first meal always right there waiting.

3-Step Packing Order

Step 1 ↓Put the MON big bag in first — goes all the way to the bottom
Step 2 ↓Put the SUN big bag in next — sits in the middle
Step 3 ↑Put the SAT big bag in last — sits on top, first thing you grab

Zip-lock bag sizes

📁 Big (gallon) — one per day, label SAT / SUN / MON
📦 Medium (quart) — one per meal inside each day bag
🔹 Small (sandwich) — one per person for individual items

Dinners and shared pouches don't need individual small bags — the whole group eats from the same pouch
